# MNFT Utility

## Overview
MNFT is designed to connect digital participation with real-world value.

## Core Utility

### Access
Holders receive:
- Early access to product drops
- Exclusive content
- Future tool access

### Rewards
MNFT is used to reward:
- Customers
- Community members
- Early supporters

### Real-World Integration
MNFT will connect to:
- Real estate opportunities
- Business ownership pathways
- Ecosystem participation

### Commerce Loop
Products → Rewards → Holding → Community → Growth

## Philosophy
Utility will be built through execution, not promises.
